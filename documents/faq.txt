Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

Q: How can I upgrade my subscription?
A: Go to Billing → Subscription and choose a new plan.

Q: How do I request a refund?
A: Submit a refund request through Billing Support. Supervisor approval is required.

Q: How can I contact support?
A: Email support@abctech.com or use Live Chat.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, and Bank Transfer.

Q: How long does refund processing take?
A: Up to 7 business days after approval.
